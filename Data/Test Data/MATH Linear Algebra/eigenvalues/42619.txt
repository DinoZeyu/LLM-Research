Problem:
Find the eigenvalues of the following matrix (round your answer to three decimal places):
$\left(
\begin{array}{ccc}
 -\frac{11}{2} & 0 & -5 \\
 \frac{5}{2} & \frac{1}{2} & -\frac{11}{2} \\
 \frac{17}{2} & \frac{17}{2} & \frac{1}{2} \\
\end{array}
\right)$.
Answer:
$\{-4.159,-0.17-9.086 i,-0.17+9.086 i\}$
